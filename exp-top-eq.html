Noon Silk (noonsilk@gmail.com), University of Melbourne.

My original abstract: We briefly review topological equivalence of knots under the Reidemeister moves and then explore extensions. We will look to understand how to add further equivalences, and discuss some of the calculi and algebras for working with these systems. This exploration will be undertaken in the context of the simplification of a particular kind of knot-like objects that arise in quantum computing architectures.
